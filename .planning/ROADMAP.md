# Roadmap: Elmar's Data & AI Training Landing Page

## Overview

Three phases take Elmar's landing page from empty scaffold to fully functional bilingual site. Phase 1 establishes the dark, responsive page structure with smooth navigation. Phase 2 delivers the visual wow factor -- the 3D hero animation and interactive expertise visualization that are the core value of the project. Phase 3 completes the page with the contact form and bilingual German/English support.

## Phase Details

### Phase 1: Page Foundation
**Goal**: Visitors see a polished, dark, futuristic single-page layout that looks great on any device and scrolls smoothly between sections
**Depends on**: Nothing (first phase)
**Requirements**: VIZ-02, VIZ-03, VIZ-04
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. Opening the page in a browser shows a dark-themed, futuristic layout with clearly defined section placeholders (hero, expertise, contact)
  2. Clicking nav links or scrolling moves smoothly between sections with no jumps or jank
  3. The page layout adapts correctly when resizing the browser from mobile (375px) to tablet (768px) to desktop (1440px) widths

### Phase 2: Visual Impact
**Goal**: Visitors are immediately impressed by an immersive 3D/particle hero animation and an interactive visualization that communicates Elmar's data & AI expertise
**Depends on**: Phase 1
**Requirements**: VIZ-01, EXP-01, EXP-02
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. The hero section displays an animated 3D or particle-based visualization themed around data and AI that runs smoothly (no stuttering or dropped frames)
  2. The expertise section presents Elmar's training areas through an animated, interactive visualization (not just static text/images)
  3. The expertise visualization communicates who Elmar is and what he offers -- a visitor can understand his focus areas without reading dense paragraphs
  4. All animations load within a reasonable time and do not break the page on mobile devices

### Phase 3: Interaction & Language
**Goal**: Visitors can contact Elmar through a form and view the entire page in either German or English
**Depends on**: Phase 2
**Requirements**: CON-01, CON-02, LANG-01, LANG-02
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. The contact section contains a form with name, email, and message fields that a visitor can fill out and submit
  2. Submitting the form with missing or invalid fields shows clear validation errors; submitting a valid form shows a success confirmation
  3. A visible language toggle allows switching between German and English
  4. After switching language, all text content on the page (nav, headings, body text, form labels, buttons) displays in the selected language
